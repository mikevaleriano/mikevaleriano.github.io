Target Audience

The target audience is described below:

  • Who: Professionals, Students and People who have a common use of technology.
  • Age: 23 - 45.
  • Techology: The users only should know how to use a browser and a smartphone or a laptop.
  • Motivation: The main motivations user is to know the weather's town where he is and to know if there is an interesting place to visit or where to eat nearby.
